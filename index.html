PROGFEST 2024

ProgFest annually showcases top-notch musicians from Colorado, presenting a mix of classic and original prog rock, art rock, jazz fusion, and related genres. The lineup features tributes to iconic bands like Rush and Genesis, along with original performances from local acts such as Every Waking Hour, Monstradaumus and Jamie Krutz & Friends.

Headlining this year is Steel Monkey, a Jethro Tull tribute band hailing from the front range.

Steven Wilson Releases Song 'December Skies' Co-written by AI

Steven Wilson has a released a new Christmas song written with the help of artificial intelligence.

According to Wilson's website, "December Skies is the result, swiftly recorded last weekend and written in collaboration with an Artificial Intelligence version of myself. I wonder what people will make of it. I wonder what I make of it.”

The visualizer for December Skies was generated using a purpose built AI system created by Miles Skarin/Crystal Spotlight.

NOVEMBER 16th 2023

Steve Hackett To Release New Album 'The Circus and the Nightwhale'

The legendary guitarist will release his latest studio album, 'The Circus and the Nightwhale' on February 16th, 2024 via InsideOut Music.

Recorded at the prestigious Siren Studio in the UK, this captivating 'rite of passage' concept album weaves the narrative of a young protagonist named Travla.

Joining Hackettt on the album are the familiar faces of Roger King, Rob Townsend, Jonas Reingold, Nad Sylvan, Craig Blundell, and Amanda Lehmann, with guest appearances by Nick D'Virgilio and Hugo Degenhardt.

MEMBER SPOTLIGHT

Seven Questions With... Liz Bathory

Where were you born?

Nyirbator, Hungary

Who was the artist(s) that got you into progressive music?

Fates Warning/Queensryche/Dream Theater

What was the first concert you went to?

U2 - The Unforgettable Fire Tour, McNichols Arena

What was the last concert you went to?

Dirty Honey. No, it's not prog. Yes, it's a dumb name. But they were the first ever unsigned act to top Billboard's Mainstream Rock Chart.

Who is your favorite 'modern' progressive artist?

Sleep Token. You can argue all day if they are progressive or not, but they're pushing the boundaries of incorporating different genres within their music. Sounds progressive to me.

If you could have dinner with any artist, living or dead, who would it be?

Steve Clark. For better or worse he was the reason I started playing guitar. Randy Rhoads would be a very close second though.

Five albums you can't live without?
  • Sleep Token - Take Me Back To Eden
  • Def Leppard - High 'n Dry
  • Queensryche - Operation Mindcrime
  • Dream Theater - Scenes From A Memory
  • Steven Wilson - Hand Cannot Erase
