PROGFEST 2024

Purchase Tickets Here

Sponsored by the Colorado Art Rock Society, each year ProgFest features some of the finest musicians in Colorado playing a variety of classic and original prog rock, art rock, jazz fusion and related musical adventures. Expect to hear tributes to classic bands like Rush and Genesis as well as originals from ground-breaking Colorado acts like Every Waking Hour Monstradaumus and Jamie Krutz & Friends.


Headlining this year is Steel Monkey, a Jethro Tull tribute band hailing from the front range.
