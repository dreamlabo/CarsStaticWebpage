PROGFEST 2024

Purchase Tickets Here

ProgFest annually showcases top-notch musicians from Colorado, presenting a mix of classic and original prog rock, art rock, jazz fusion, and related genres. The lineup features tributes to iconic bands like Rush and Genesis, along with original performances from local acts such as Every Waking Hour, Monstradaumus and Jamie Krutz & Friends.

Headlining this year is Steel Monkey, a Jethro Tull tribute band hailing from the front range.
